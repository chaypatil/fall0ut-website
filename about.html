About FallØut

Built from the underground

FallØut is India's premier underground rave collective. It connects the people, rooms and nights pushing the country's harder edge forward.

Rave Calendar FØ Moments

01

The movement

FallØut began with a simple instinct: the underground should be documented by people inside it. The platform brings current dates, direct event access and the memory of past nights into one place without sanding down the culture that made them matter.

02

What stays central

Sound, crowd and room come first. The calendar follows what is live, permanent event pages preserve what has passed, and FØ Moments holds the photographs selected from the nights worth returning to.
