The sound of FallØut

Scene language

Hard techno
Fast, physical techno built around heavy kicks, pressure and high-energy rooms.
Schranz
Relentless, percussive hard techno with distorted drums and forward momentum.
Industrial
Metallic textures, darker atmospheres and raw machine-driven impact.
Hard groove
Rolling percussion and body-led rhythm with speed, swing and functional energy.
Hard bounce
High-speed grooves, elastic low end and direct dancefloor release.
Warehouse rave
Raw, immersive rave energy built around sound, community and the room itself.
